Welcome to The Comp Sci Club
What we do...

We do comp sci stuff, like coding, debugging, and pretending we know what we're doing. It's basically magic, but with more caffine and fewer wands.

Sometimes we even build things that work! Other times, we just stare at error messages until they go away (spoiler: they don't).

But hey, at least we have fun while we're doing it! And if all else fails, we can always blame the computer.

So if you're looking for a place to learn, grow, and maybe even break a few things along the way, you've come to the right place.

Join us for programming competitions, games, and free food.

Whether you're a coding wizard or just starting out, there's something for everyone!
